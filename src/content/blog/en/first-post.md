This is my first blog post. I'll talk about why I decided to build this personal site and the thinking behind my tech choices.

## Why a Personal Site

In an era of social media overload, having a digital space of your own matters more than ever. No algorithms, no platform constraints — full control over content and presentation.

## Tech Stack

I chose **Astro** as the static site generator for these reasons:

- **Zero JS by default**: Pure HTML/CSS output, excellent performance
- **Content Collections**: Smooth Markdown writing experience
- **Built-in i18n**: Native bilingual routing support
- **Minimalist philosophy**: No unnecessary abstractions
